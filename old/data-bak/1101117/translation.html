Again in the year 555 of the Armenian era Chokurmish, the emir of Mosul, died at the hands of Jawali the Persian emir. because there was fierce battle on both sides. and Jawali defeated the army of Chokurmish. and struck him a deadly wound with an arrow. And after a few days that evil beast perished. and he gave all his land to the sultan Kilij Arslan. He also delivered the count Baldwin, the count of Edessa, into his hands, because he had taken Baldwin as his servant. And Kilij Arslan, the sultan of the West, collected troops and, going forth, took Mosul and Jazira and all his land. In this year, before this, Kilij Arslan came with many troops against the city of Edessa, and remained there a few days, making several attacks. and unable to do anything, he arose and went and took the city called Harran, and he put the entire land under his power, and returned to his own land.
